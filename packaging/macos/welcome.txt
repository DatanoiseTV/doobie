Doobie — analog dub delay

This installer adds the following formats to your system:

  • Audio Unit (AU)  -> /Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/Components
  • VST3             -> /Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/VST3
  • Standalone app   -> /Applications

You can deselect individual components on the next screen if you only
want one or two formats.

Requires macOS 11 (Big Sur) or newer. Universal binary — runs natively
on Apple silicon and Intel.
